Teacher & Parent Notes

Topic: Angle of Depression (Class 10)
NCERT: Ch. 9, Exercise 9.1
Concepts: Angle of depression = angle of elevation, tan ratios, horizontal distance

Learning Objectives

  • Understand angle of depression from horizontal line
  • Apply alternate interior angles (depression = elevation)
  • Use tan θ = opposite/adjacent in two right triangles
  • Solve for unknown height using two angle conditions

Common Errors

  • Confusing angle of depression with angle from vertical
  • Not using alternate interior angles to convert
  • Using sin/cos instead of tan for horizontal distance
  • Subtracting angles (60°−30°=30°) instead of using tan
  • Forgetting that depression to top gives smaller triangle
